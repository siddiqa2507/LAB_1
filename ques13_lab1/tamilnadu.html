Ongoing Developments

Tamil Nadu is expanding infrastructure including highways, ports, and IT parks. The state is also focusing on renewable energy and manufacturing growth.

Importance of the State

Tamil Nadu is one of India's most industrialized states. It is a leader in automobile manufacturing, textiles, and IT services.
